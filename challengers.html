Custom Molded Stereo Challenger™ Series Earphones

The Generation I and Generation II Challenger "C" were designed as a monitor for the person who wants a stereo or bin-aural sound mix piped directly into his or her ear canals. Our custom molds are perfect for high noise communications applications.  Not for the faint hearted, the Challenger "C" Monitor Earphone features dynamic sub miniature transducers and is used for monitoring onstage as well as listening enjoyment during all forms of activity.

The Generation I Challenger is contained in our JB1000 silicone material. JB 1000 is a medical grade silicone material that is Super Soft and provides a complete seal of the ear canals from outside noise. The full helix earmold design of the Challenger "C" provides a flexible water resistant seal of the ear canals that allow affordable, customized high quality audio to go wherever you go. The Generation I design comes with a 52” stereo cord and blocks up to 28Db of ambient noise. This model can be used for more active use such as jogging, gym, motorcycling or even multimedia use.

The Generation II Challenger is contained in our Lucite acrylic material. Lucite is a light hypoallergenic acrylic material that provides a comfortable fit in the ear. This light design helps give the feeling as if there wasn’t anything in the ear at all. The Generation II model comes with a 42” stereo cord with a built in microphone and blocks out up to 22Db of ambient noise. This more elegant polished look is meant for those who wish to use their earphones as both a listening and a communication device in more of a business manner.

3. If you need ear impressions made please utilize our audiologist search engine to locate an audiologist in your area to get your ear impressions made. Once you have the ear impressions they can be sent to us for your molds.
