#24 Open-fit Custom Earpiece - If given a choice, which would you prefer?

#24 Open-Fit

Why not offer this value added feature to your portfolio of products?

Our research shows that 30% of B.T.E fittings are the open fit design that could benefit from our  "custom" ear pieces.

Precision Laboratories has announced their version of the open fitting custom earpiece. The unique design allows the maximum in comfort and cosmetics. 

The #24 open fit may be ordered in soft and hard materials and will couple to any open fitting hearing device. The unique design can also be ordered with the select-a-vent which allows even more flexibility.
